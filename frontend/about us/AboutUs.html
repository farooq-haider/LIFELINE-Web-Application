About Us

LIFELINE is a purpose-built platform aiming to connect blood donors with recipients across Pakistan. Our journey is fueled by passion, empathy, and innovation to save lives.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Our Vision

Transforming Blood Donation Accessibility

We envision a world where finding a life-saving blood donor is just a click away — no delays, no uncertainty, just hope and action.

Our Mission

Technology for Humanity

To build a secure, smart, and compassionate ecosystem that brings together verified donors and recipients in minutes, not hours.

The Team

Developers on a Mission

A team of student developers, united by empathy and driven by purpose, who came together to build more than just a semester project — a true lifeline.

Core Values

What We Stand For

Transparency. Innovation. Security. Empathy. These values are our foundation and guide every decision we make to improve the user experience and save more lives.
